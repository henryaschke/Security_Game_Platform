Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my cybersecurity training?

Simply click on any training module from the dashboard. We recommend starting with the Password Security module for beginners. Each module includes interactive games, assessments, and real-world scenarios to help you learn effectively.

What happens if I fail a phishing detection test?

Don't worry! Failing is part of learning. You'll receive immediate feedback explaining what you missed, along with tips to improve. You can retake the test as many times as needed to master the skills.

How is my progress tracked?

CyberSmart automatically tracks your completion rates, scores, time spent, and achievements. You can view detailed analytics in the Progress section of your dashboard, including strengths and areas for improvement.

Can I access CyberSmart on mobile devices?

Yes! CyberSmart is fully responsive and works on tablets and smartphones. While some interactive elements work best on desktop, all core training features are accessible on mobile devices.

How long does each training module take?

Training modules vary in length: Password Security (15-20 min), Phishing Detection (25-30 min), and Skill Assessments (30-45 min). You can pause and resume at any time, so you can learn at your own pace.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes! Upon successfully completing all modules and assessments, you'll receive a digital certificate that demonstrates your cybersecurity knowledge. This can be shared on LinkedIn or included in your professional portfolio.
